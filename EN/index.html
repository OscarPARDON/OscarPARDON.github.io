Who is Oscar Pardon ?

My journey in computer science started very early. From middle school, I was creating popular games within the school using Scratch. In high school, I discovered web development with HTML, CSS and JavaScript, which strengthened my interest in this field. I then chose the digital and computer science specialty for my baccalaureate, which allowed me to acquire solid foundations in computer science, particularly in Python.

After obtaining my baccalaureate, it seemed natural to continue with a BTS SIO, SLAM option. During these two years, completed in work-study at AFIP Formations and in company at the Mutualité Française de Haute-Saône, I alternated between theoretical learning, academic projects, professional missions and personal projects. This experience allowed me to progress quickly and exceed academic expectations.

Self-taught by nature and soon to be training for a Bachelor's degree in Software Developement, I actively continue developing my skills, whether through my courses, my business experience or my personal projects.
